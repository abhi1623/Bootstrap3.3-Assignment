Campaigns

Let Her Fly 2015

On Jan 24th, 2015 on National Girl Child Day we launched our online fundraising campaign ‘Let Her Fly’ on the issue of rights of the girl child. The campaign aimed to inspire and encourage people to give the girl child the opportunities that she so rightly deserves.

Learn more

Child Rights 2015

Our '#OpenYourEyes' photojournalism campaign in 2014 was on the issue of child labour. The campaign was an attempt to sensitise citizens- and duty-bearers- out of their inertia and open their eyes to the grim reality of child labour.

Learn more

Happy First Birthday

Our ‘Happy First Birthday’ initiative was aimed at ensuring 42,971 infants were provided access to the lifesaving vaccines they needed to celebrate their first birthday. With thousands of people joining us in our efforts to gift children a birthday for their first birthday, we ensured over 44,200 children had access to immunisation and a healthy start to life.

Learn more

Project Unlearn 2014-15

On Nov 14th, 2014 we launched our online fundraising campaign Project Unlearn on the issue of child labour. The campaign urged people to donate to ensure 28,588 children from CRY-supported projects are removed from the clutches of child labour, put back into schools and unlearn those skills which rob them of their childhood.

Learn more

J&K floods 2014

In Jammu and Kashmir, battling one of the worst floods in decades, CRY and its partners are working with the local government and agencies to provide critical support and relief to families and children. Click here to know more about our efforts in affected areas.

Learn more

Art For Cry 2015

Paritosh Sen, Prakash Karmakar, Rabin Mondal were just some of the artists whose works was a part of a week long exhibition and sale of paintings and sculptures in Kolkata in November 2014 for the cause of #childrights.

Learn more

Mission Education 2013

CRY launched the year-long target driven online fundraising campaign Mission Education towards the issues that keep children out of school, like migration, lack of access to schools and girl child discrimination. We enrolled 4,60,000 children in schools and ensured their right to a happy and creative childhood.

Learn more

Marathon 2013

The Standard Chartered Marathon is Mumbai's largest annual fundraising public event. People from all walks of life joined CRY to run the marathon and support child rights.

Learn more

Uttarakhand Floods 2013

In the aftermath of the torrential rains in Uttarakhand in June 2013 thousands of people were stranded and hundreds of children were left traumatised. CRY along with its partner NGO's in Uttarakhand worked relentlessly to provide a safe haven for children.

Learn more
